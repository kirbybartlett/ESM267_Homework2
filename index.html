Bull Trout Range Represented by Habitat Use Type

The purpose of the range map is to create a visual representation for the current known and inferred distribution of Salvelinus confluentus (Bull Trout). Current range maps form the basis for Area of Influence maps that are used in the Information, Planning and Consultation (IPaC) system for the conservation of Endangered Species Act listed species. Area of Influence maps enable users to search to see where listed species occur in conjunction with critical habitat to determine if a section 7 consultation is necessary.
